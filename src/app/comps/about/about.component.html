Our Story



THE SciBOX is a medical writing organisation. It was founded in the year 2020 by our respectable founder and CEO Mr. Fahad Ur Rehman. He later teamed up with his friend Tahaa Sohail to take this organisation to the apex of medical writing realm.

Inception



It all started when Mr. Fahad Ur Rehman started medical writing as a freelancer on Fiverr. He started it as a side hustle. Along his path he realized that medical writing is something that is in high demand in the global market and is expensive too. So it is then that he founded THE SciBOX in the year 2020. An organization where clients can get proficient medical writers at sensible rates. THE SciBOX’s first venture was Upwork and it did exceptionally well there. Earned “Rising Talent” badge and 100% Job Success Score in no time.
Evaluation of the market revealed to us that there are levels of medical writing. Not all is strenuous as the name medical implies. This is why we provide medical writers for the small and not-so-demanding tasks. Senior medical writers for the strenuous jobs. And teams for complex jobs.
